What Gets Covered
5 Pillars. One score.

These five pillars are the axes of your Gridiron IQ radar chart — every lesson and quiz question maps to one. Train a pillar here, watch it grow on your IQ exam.

01
Rules & Objectives

Scoring, downs, penalties, turnovers, and how the game works from kickoff to final whistle.

02
Positions & Responsibilities

Every position on offense, defense, and special teams — what they do and why it matters.

03
Formations & Alignments

How teams line up, why spacing matters, and what pre-snap alignment tells you.

04
Plays & Concepts

Routes, coverages, run schemes, blitzes, and how plays attack and defend.

05
Situational Awareness

3rd down, red zone, clock management, and thinking under game pressure.
